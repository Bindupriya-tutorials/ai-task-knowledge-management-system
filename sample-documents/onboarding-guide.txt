Employee Onboarding Guide

New hires should complete the following steps in their first week:
1. Set up your laptop and install the company VPN client.
2. Complete the mandatory security and compliance training modules.
3. Meet with your assigned mentor to review team goals and workflows.
4. Request access to the internal knowledge base and project management tools.
5. Schedule a one-on-one with your manager to discuss the 30-60-90 day plan.

IT support can be reached at it-support@company.com for any access issues.
HR handles benefits enrollment, which must be completed within 14 days of the start date.
