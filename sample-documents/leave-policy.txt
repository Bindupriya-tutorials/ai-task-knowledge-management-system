Company Leave Policy

Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid time off per year, credited monthly.
Sick leave is separate and provides 10 days annually for personal illness or
caring for an immediate family member. Unused PTO up to 5 days can be carried
over into the next calendar year; anything beyond that is forfeited.

To request leave, submit a request through the HR portal at least 5 business
days in advance for planned leave. Emergency leave should be reported to your
manager as soon as possible. Parental leave policies are detailed in a separate
document available from HR.
