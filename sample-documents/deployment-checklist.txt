Production Deployment Checklist

Before deploying to production, engineers must confirm the following:
- All unit and integration tests pass in the CI pipeline.
- Database migrations have been reviewed and tested on staging.
- Feature flags are configured correctly for gradual rollout.
- Rollback plan is documented and the previous build artifact is retained.
- On-call engineer is notified of the deployment window.
- Monitoring dashboards and alerts are verified after deployment completes.

Any deployment outside business hours requires sign-off from the engineering lead.
